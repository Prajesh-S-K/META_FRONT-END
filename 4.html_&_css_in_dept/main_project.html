About Little Lemon

Based in Chicago, Illinois, Little Lemon is a family-owned Mediterranean restaurant, focused on traditional recipes served with a modern twist. With a rustic and relaxed atmosphere, moderate prices, and a welcoming vibe, Little Lemon is a popular choice for any meal of the day.

Little Lemon is owned by two Italian brothers, Mario and Adrian, who moved to the United States to pursue their shared dream of owning a restaurant. Mario crafts the menu using family recipes and his experience as a chef in Italy, while Adrian manages marketing and expanding the menu to incorporate Mediterranean cuisines.
